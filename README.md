# RNN — Real News Network 🌍✨

**RNN (Real News Network)** is an independent, AI-assisted news prototype dedicated to sharing **uplifting, positive, and truthful stories** from around the world.  
Where traditional media often focuses on fear and negativity, RNN highlights the good — kindness, breakthroughs, cultural beauty, and the resilience of Earth.

---

## 🔥 What You’ll Find
- **Breaking News:** A top story that updates frequently.  
- **Recent Stories:** A curated selection of uplifting global news.  
- **Categories:** Health, Earth, Kindness, Breakthroughs, Culture, and more.  
- **Archive:** Past stories stored for reference and inspiration.

---

## 🚀 Vision
RNN is the spark for a new kind of network:  
- **No misinformation.**  
- **No noise.**  
- Only *real news that uplifts humanity*.  

The dream: one day, RNN can grow into a full network with contributors worldwide, reminding us daily that good things are happening everywhere.
